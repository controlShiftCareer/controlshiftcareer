Control Shift Career FAQ

Who should buy this package?

Are you a final year student or a recent graduate with outstanding skills but struggling to land your dream job? Maybe you have some outstanding skills but haven't learned how to showcase them effectively. Our packages are designed to help students like you build confidence and crack interviews by crafting a powerful resume and developing essential interview skills.

Why choose Control Shift Career over simply copying a sample resume?

While copying a sample resume might seem like a quick fix, it has limitations. Those skills might not match your own experience, and recruiters are looking for candidates with up-to-date skills relevant to the current job market. Your resume is your first impression, and a generic copy is unlikely to stand out. We help you craft a personalised resume that highlights your unique qualifications and aligns with your desired career path. This ensures a confident start and sets you apart from the competition.

Free AI tools vs. Control Shift Career's expert guidance:

AI resume builders can be a handy starting point, but they can't guarantee a job offer. A resume needs to stand out, and you need the skills to back it up. Our experts go beyond a basic resume. We analyse market trends, tailor your resume to showcase relevant skills, and prepare you to demonstrate your capabilities during interviews. We equip you with the knowledge and confidence to become a highly sought-after candidate.

What does "support validity" mean?

We believe in empowering students, not just handing them answers. Our packages offer varying support periods (3, 5, or 21 days) to ensure you receive personalised guidance every step of the way. You'll learn how to independently refine your profile, not just rely on one-time assistance. We provide daily programs to help you progress systematically. Our 21-day program is a comprehensive experience, simulating a real-world work environment with daily tasks and instructions. This prepares you for the demands of the job market and equips you to handle challenging interview scenarios.

Do you provide answers to your question bank?

Yes, we offer access to an answer book (additional cost) containing solutions to the most common interview questions using the STAR technique. However, unlocking this benefit requires completing 80% of the course. This ensures you've invested the time and effort to understand the concepts before relying solely on answers. Trust us, by actively learning to answer the questions yourself, you'll gain the knowledge and confidence to ace any interview. Remember, our question bank covers a wide range, but it's your responsibility to apply the skills and knowledge we provide to excel.

Do you offer dedicated coaching?

Yes! We offer specialised coaching programs for high-demand fields like Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Machine Learning, Data Analytics, and programming languages like Python and Bash Scripting. These programs are typically offered from August to April. However, we can provide Python coding training upon request. Simply contact careersupos for details on our Python course and guidance.

Control Shift Career Referral Program FAQ

How does the referral program work?

Purchase a Qualifying Package: Enrol in one of our career-boosting packages: RESUME, INTERVIEW, or LOADED.
Receive Unique ID: Upon purchase, you'll get an invoice containing your unique enrolment ID.
Share Your ID: Encourage friends to use your ID when they enroll in any of our packages.
Friend Benefits: Your friend receives a 10% cash-back on their chosen package. Referral cashback earned by your friend will be credited to their account along with their referral commissions.
Your Commissions: Earn a commission ranging from 25% to 35% based on your purchased package. Commissions are paid according to the schedule below.

What are the commission payout schedules?

RESUME Package: Commissions are paid on Monday, Wednesday, or Friday. Your first commission payment determines the recurring payout day for future commissions.
INTERVIEW Package: Commissions are paid on Tuesday or Thursday. Similar to the RESUME package, the day of your first commission will become your regular payout day.
LOADED Package: Commissions for the LOADED package are paid every Saturday.

How soon will my friend receive their cash-back?

Referral cashback earned by your friend will be credited to their account along with their referral commissions.

Is there a limit to how many friends I can refer?

No, there's no limit to the number of friends you can refer. The more you refer, the more you can earn and develop your communication skills!

How can I track my referrals and commissions?

We'll be implementing a dedicated referral dashboard soon where you can track your referrals, commission payouts, and communication tools to connect with potential referrals.
